Hello!

I am Raisa Gandi Putri.

I was created and packaged in Jakarta, Indonesia. Then, I was exported to California and re-packaged even further. I majored in Computer Science and minored in Music at the University of California, Davis, and completed my education in a little over three years.

I was then shipped to Massachusetts, where I worked at Bose's Alexa Voice Cloud Team. There, I stretched my abilities as an engineer, and I was an apprentice to the most amazing and accomplished mentors who have now become my friends. I may be re-packaged or shipped to other destinations for the better, but one thing remains certain: I will always remain an apprentice. While I may evolve and move on to other destinations in this world, the relationships that I've built along the way are as permanent as I want them to be.

Along the journey in my quest for knowledge, I've left breadcrumbs through music. I started composing since I was thirteen years old - here's the first song I wrote and composed! I've been composing throughout high school and college as well. My primary instrument for music composition is the piano, but I occasionally sing, play violin, and guitar. I ventured from pop/singer-songwriter style in high school, to contemporary piano instrumentals in college, and finally I broke my artistic barriers while studying Electronic Music in my senior year of college where I composed avant-garde pieces like this.

Please check out my originals and covers on my SoundCloud!
